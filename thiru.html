THIRUVANANTHAPURAM

THIRUVANANTHAPURAM - THE CAPITAL CITY OF KERALA

Thiruvananthapuram, the capital of Kerala, is a perfect blend of heritage, spirituality, beaches, and modernity. Nestled along the Arabian Sea, the city is known for its timeless charm and vibrant culture. From the ancient Padmanabhaswamy Temple to the golden sands of Kovalam Beach, it offers something for every traveler.

Tourist Attractions

  • Padmanabhaswamy Temple – One of the richest temples in the world, known for its architectural grandeur and spiritual importance.
  • Kovalam Beach – Ideal for sunbathing, swimming, and surfing. The lighthouse beach is a major attraction.
  • Napier Museum & Art Gallery – Explore Kerala’s rich history, art, and archaeological artifacts.
  • Ponmudi Hill Station – A serene getaway with tea plantations, misty hills, and trekking trails.
  • Veli Tourist Village – Offers boating, children’s park, and floating café experiences.

Unique Experiences

  • Watch a Kathakali performance at a cultural center in the city.
  • Visit Magic Planet in Kazhakkoottam – India’s first magic-themed park.
  • Take a heritage walk in the old city area near East Fort.
  • Enjoy traditional Kerala cuisine at iconic restaurants like Mothers Veg Plaza and Villa Maya.

Foods to Try

  • Kerala Sadhya (traditional feast)
  • Neyyappam (ghee-fried sweet)
  • Fish Molee with Appam
  • Tapioca with spicy Kerala beef curry
